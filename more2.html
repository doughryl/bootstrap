Getting Started

Configure your wallet

After downloading your chosen wallet, you can configure it according to our step-by-step guide. Here are some key steps to consider:

Create a new wallet:
If you're using a software wallet like MetaMask or Trust Wallet, you'll need to create a new wallet. Follow the wallet's instructions to generate a secure wallet address.

Secure your wallet:
Enable security features such as PIN codes, biometric authentication, or two-factor authentication (2FA) to protect your Cat Coin holdings.

Add Cat Coin:
Add Cat Coin to your wallet by inputting the contract address or scanning the QR code from our website. Ensure that you have the correct token details to avoid any issues.

Backup your wallet:
Always back up your wallet's recovery phrase or private keys. Store this information in a secure and offline location. It's crucial for recovering your Cat Coin in case of wallet loss or damage.
