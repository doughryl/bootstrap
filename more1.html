Getting Started

Choose your wallet

A wallet is necessary for people to use, trade, or hold Cat Coin. You can pick a wallet that suits your needs. Here are some options:

MetaMask:
MetaMask is a popular Ethereum wallet that supports Cat Coin. It's a browser extension wallet that offers both security and ease of use. You can download it from the official website and add the Cat Coin token to your wallet.

Trust Wallet:
Trust Wallet is a mobile wallet available on both Android and iOS devices. It's user-friendly and allows you to store Cat Coin securely on your smartphone.

Ledger Nano S:
For those who prioritize security, a hardware wallet like the Ledger Nano S is an excellent choice. It stores Cat Coin offline, making it resistant to hacking attempts.

Once you've chosen a wallet, make sure to keep it secure and back up your private keys or recovery phrase in a safe place.
